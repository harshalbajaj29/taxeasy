FAQ

Questions businesses ask us

Do I need to know accounting to use Taxnio?+
No. Taxnio is built for business owners, not accountants. You just upload your bank statement and the AI does everything. No accounting knowledge, no Tally training, no manual entry required.
Which banks are supported?+
All major Indian banks — HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank, IndusInd, PNB and more. Any bank that provides PDF or CSV statements works with Taxnio instantly.
How accurate is the AI at reading receipts and transactions?+
Over 95% accuracy on standard bank statements and receipts. For anything the AI is unsure about, it puts it in "Others Review" so you can confirm with one click. The AI learns from your corrections every month.
Is my financial data safe and private?+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share or sell your data. Your financial information is only accessible by you.
Do I still need a CA if I use Taxnio?+
Taxnio handles day-to-day bookkeeping and basic tax questions. For complex tax planning, GST audits and ITR filing, you still need a CA — but Taxnio gives them everything organised, so their work is faster and cheaper for you.
What happens after the free month?+
You choose a plan and pay monthly. No long term contracts. Cancel anytime. We offer a 100% money-back guarantee if you are not satisfied.
Can I export data to Tally or share with my CA?+
Yes. Growth and Enterprise plans include Tally-compatible XML export. You can also export all transactions as CSV and share directly with your CA.
