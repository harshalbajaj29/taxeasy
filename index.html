FAQ

Questions CA firms ask us

Do my clients need to download any app? +
No. Your clients use WhatsApp — the app already on their phone. They just send receipts and messages to your dedicated Taxnio number. Zero friction, zero downloads, zero training required.
How accurate is the AI at reading receipts? +
Our AI uses Claude — the same technology trusted by Fortune 500 companies — specifically tuned for Indian receipts with mixed Hindi and English text. It handles handwritten receipts, blurry photos and multiple formats. Accuracy is above 95% for standard receipts.
Is my client data safe and private? +
Absolutely. All data is stored securely on Indian servers. Each CA firm's data is completely isolated — no other CA can see your clients. We never share or sell your data. Only registered clients can interact with your WhatsApp number.
How long does setup take? +
Most CA firms are live in under 10 minutes. Sign up, get your WhatsApp number, add your clients, share the number — done. No technical knowledge required.
Can I export data to Tally? +
Yes. Growth and Pro plans include Tally-compatible XML export. You can export all receipts and invoices in Tally format with one click. Direct Tally integration is available on the Pro plan.
What happens after the free month? +
After your free first month you choose a plan and pay monthly. No long term contracts. Cancel anytime — your data is always exportable. We will never hold your data hostage.
Does it work for CA firms with multiple partners? +
Yes. The Growth plan supports team access. The Pro plan includes full multi-user access with different permission levels for partners, managers and junior staff.
